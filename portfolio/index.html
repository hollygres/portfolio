Product Design

I Need an A 2022 – Current

After the fall of Roe v. Wade, I got involved with I Need an A, a tech non-profit that helps folks access abortion care in an increasingly legally restrictive environment. We help abortion seekers across the US navigate the confusing maze of providers, laws, and funding and support options through up-to-date information in an easy-to-use product. I stepped into a larger role in fall 2024, leading design across product, brand, and content as part of a small cross-functional team. Over the last year, I’ve designed a private, anonymous web chat application, an abortion cost estimator, a data management system, and brand and social experiences, while continuing to refine our existing search-based interface.
